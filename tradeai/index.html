How TradeAI routes your factory:

If your monthly container output is ≥ 1, your factory is eligible for direct routing to a registered retailer order. If < 1 container, TradeAI automatically pools your output with nearby factories in the same grid (Tea/Coffee Board branch cluster) to form a full container for export.
